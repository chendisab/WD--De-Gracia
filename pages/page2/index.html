NICO

ROBIN

Nico Robin, an archaeologist in "One Piece" and member of the Straw Hat Pirates, can read ancient Poneglyphs and possesses the Hana Hana no Mi (Flower-Flower Fruit). This Devil Fruit allows her to sprout multiple copies of her body parts on any surface, providing versatility in combat and utility. She can create extra limbs for various purposes, making her a formidable and resourceful crew member, combining intellect with her unique abilities.
