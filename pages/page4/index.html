FAIRYTAIL

LUCY HEARTFILIA

Celestial Princess.

Lucy Heartfilia, a key character in "Fairy Tail," is a Celestial Spirit Mage who wields magical keys to summon powerful celestial spirits. Each spirit has unique abilities, aiding Lucy in both combat and support roles. Her most notable spirits include Aquarius, a water bearer; Leo, a powerful fighter; and Virgo, an earth manipulator. Over time, Lucy's bond with her spirits strengthens, enhancing their power and teamwork. In addition to her summoning skills, Lucy also excels in whip-based combat and has developed some impressive magical abilities of her own, making her a versatile and resourceful mage in the Fairy Tail guild.

Skill 1

Using her golden keys she summons celestial spirit to fight beside her.

Skill 2

User her strong magic power she can share her spirits ability which can make her more stronger
